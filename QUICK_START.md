## Getting Started

### 1. Launch the App
Open CosmIQ Guru. You'll see the cosmic splash screen with twinkling stars and the app title.

### 2. Set Up Your Profile
The app will walk you through 3 simple steps:

**Step 1 - Your Name**
Enter your full name. This is used for numerology calculations.

**Step 2 - Birth Details**
- **Date of Birth** - Tap the calendar to select
- **Birth Time** - Tap the clock to select (as accurate as possible)
- **Birth City** - Type your city and country of birth

**Step 3 - Personality Quiz**
Answer 12 quick questions to determine your Jungian Archetype. Tap the answer that feels most like you — the app auto-advances after each selection.

After the quiz, the app maps your cosmic blueprint across 7 divination systems. This takes a few seconds.

### 3. You're In!
You land on your **Home Dashboard** with your daily cosmic reading ready.

## Key Features at a Glance

### Daily Luck Score
The circular gauge on Home shows your composite luck score (0-100) calculated from 7 systems. Tap it for a full breakdown.

### Energy Meters
Three bars showing your **Relationship**, **Money**, and **Career** energy for the day. Tap any bar for a detailed explanation.

### Decision Engine
Go to the **Decide** tab, type your question, pick a category, and tap "Consult the Cosmos" for a scored recommendation with timing advice.

### Compatibility Check
In the **Match** tab, enter someone's name and birth details to see romantic or business compatibility across 5 systems.

### Money Mode
Tap the "Money Mode" card on Home for lucky numbers, investment mood, 7-day forecast, wealth cycle, and business timing calendar.

### Journal
Tap the "Journal" card on Home to log daily entries with mood tracking and cosmic notes.

### Streak System
Open the app daily to build your streak! Milestone celebrations at 7, 30, and 100 days.
