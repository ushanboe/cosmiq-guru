## 1. Overview

CosmIQ Guru is a mystical life operating system that combines **7 ancient divination systems** into a unified daily guidance dashboard. The app calculates personalized cosmic readings based on your birth details and personality profile, providing actionable insights for decisions, relationships, finances, and daily life.

### The 7 Systems
| System | Weight | What It Analyses |
|--------|--------|-----------------|
| Astrology | 18% | Sun, Moon, and Rising signs from your birth chart |
| Numerology | 18% | Life Path, Expression, Soul Urge, and Personality numbers |
| Chinese Zodiac | 13% | Animal sign, element, Yin/Yang, inner and secret animals |
| Mahabote | 17% | Burmese astrology — birth day, animal, planet, direction |
| Lunar Cycles | 14% | Current moon phase influence on daily activities |
| Archetypes | 10% | Your Jungian personality archetype from the onboarding quiz |
| Planetary Hours | 10% | 24-hour planetary cycle determining best activity windows |

All scores are weighted and combined into a single **Composite Luck Score** (0-100) that drives your daily readings.

## 2. Getting Started

### First Launch
When you open CosmIQ Guru for the first time, you'll be guided through a 3-step onboarding process.

### Step 1: Your Name
Enter your **full name** as it appears on your birth certificate. This is important because numerology calculations are based on the letter values in your name. Your Expression Number, Soul Urge Number, and Personality Number all derive from your name.

- Type your full name in the text field
- The "Continue" button activates once you've entered a name
- Tap "Continue" to proceed

### Step 2: Birth Coordinates
Three pieces of information are needed to map your cosmic blueprint:

**Date of Birth**
- Tap the calendar card to open the date picker
- Scroll to find your birth year, month, and day
- This determines your zodiac signs, Chinese zodiac animal, life path number, and Mahabote profile

**Birth Time**
- Tap the clock card to open the time picker
- Select the hour and minute as accurately as possible
- Birth time affects your Rising Sign (Ascendant) and Planetary Hour calculations
- If you don't know your exact birth time, use 12:00 PM as an approximation

**Birth City/Country**
- Type your city and country of birth (e.g., "Sydney, Australia")
- This is stored for future timezone and location-based calculations

Tap "Continue" when all three fields are filled.

### Step 3: Personality Quiz
A 12-question multiple-choice quiz determines your **Jungian Archetype** — one of 12 personality patterns that influence your cosmic readings.

**How it works:**
- Each question presents 4 options in a 2x2 grid
- Tap the option that resonates most with you
- The selected card highlights in purple with a gold glow
- After a brief pause, the quiz auto-advances to the next question
- A progress bar at the top tracks your position (Q1/12 through Q12/12)
- You can tap the back arrow to revisit and change previous answers

**The 12 Archetypes:**
Hero, Sage, Explorer, Outlaw, Magician, Innocent, Creator, Ruler, Caregiver, Lover, Jester, Everyman

Your answers are scored across all 12 archetypes simultaneously. The archetype with the highest score becomes your **lead archetype**, which influences Decision Engine recommendations and Compatibility Analysis.

### Profile Calculation
After the quiz, a loading screen appears while the app:
- Calculates your full zodiac profile (Sun, Moon, Rising signs)
- Determines your Chinese Zodiac animal, element, and companions
- Computes your Mahabote day, animal, planet, and direction
- Derives your numerology numbers (Life Path, Expression, Soul Urge, Personality)
- Detects Master Numbers (11, 22, 33) if applicable
- Saves everything to local storage

This takes a few seconds. You'll see cycling messages like "Reading the stars...", "Calculating your numbers...", and "Aligning the planets..." while the app works.

Once complete, you're taken to the main app.

The Home screen is your daily cosmic command center. It refreshes each day with new readings based on the current planetary positions, lunar phase, and your personal numerology cycles.

**Tap any meter** to open a bottom sheet with a detailed explanation of what's driving that energy level today.

### Lucky Details
A split card showing:
- **Lucky Colour** — derived from today's planetary hour ruler

Four cards providing shortcuts to key features:

| Card | Destination |

Swipe down from the top of the Home screen to regenerate all readings for the current day.

## 4. Decision Engine

Access via the **Decide** tab (second icon in the bottom navigation).

The Decision Engine consults all 7 divination systems to score any life decision you're considering.

### How to Use

1. **Type your question** — Be specific (e.g., "Should I accept the job offer from Company X?" rather than just "job")
2. **Select a category** — Choose from 8 categories:
   - Business & Career
   - Money & Investment
   - Love & Relationships
   - Travel
   - Health & Wellness
   - Education
   - Property & Real Estate
   - General
3. **Pick a target date** — When you're planning to act (today through 1 year ahead)
4. **Tap "Consult the Cosmos"**

### Reading the Results

**Cosmic Verdict Score** (0-100)
- The large central number shows how cosmically aligned your decision is
- Red (0-39): Unfavourable — consider waiting or alternatives
- Gold (40-69): Mixed signals — proceed with caution
- Green (70-100): Highly favourable — the cosmos supports this

**Detail Chips:**
- **Best Window** — Optimal time of day to act (planetary hour)
- **Lucky Number** — Your lucky number for this decision
- **Lucky Colour** — Colour to wear or surround yourself with
- **Lucky Direction** — Face this compass direction when deciding
- **Risk Level** — Low, Medium, or High (colour-coded)
- **Category** — The category you selected

**System Breakdown:**
Individual scores from each of the 7 divination systems, shown as labelled progress bars. This helps you understand which systems support the decision and which advise caution.

**Cosmic Advice:**
A personalised narrative considering your archetype, the decision category, risk level, and cosmic alignment. This section may take a moment to generate.

### Decision History
Below the results, you'll see a list of your past decisions with scores and dates. **Swipe left** on any entry to delete it.

## 6. Compatibility Analysis (Match)

Access via the **Match** tab (fourth icon in the bottom navigation).

Analyse romantic or business compatibility with anyone using their birth details.

Analyses love compatibility across 5 systems: Astrology, Numerology, Chinese Zodiac, Mahabote, and Lunar Phase.

**Business Mode**
Analyses professional compatibility with an additional **Partnership Type** selector:
- Co-Founder — Equal partnership dynamics
- Employee — Employer-employee fit
- Partner — Business partnership harmony
- Investor — Financial relationship alignment

Business mode uses Expression Number Synergy, Life Path Alignment, Chinese Zodiac Fit, Mahabote Compatibility, and Archetype Partnership scoring.

### How to Use

1. **Select mode** — Romantic or Business toggle at the top
2. **Enter partner's name** — Their full name
3. **Select their date of birth** — Tap the calendar card
4. **Select their birth time** — Tap the clock card (use 12:00 PM if unknown)
5. **Tap "Calculate Compatibility"**

**Score Circle**
A large circular gauge showing the overall compatibility percentage:
- Red (0-39%): Challenging match
- Gold (40-69%): Moderate compatibility
- Green (70-100%): Strong match

**System Scores**
Five detailed sections showing compatibility scores per system, each with a description explaining the dynamic between you and the other person.

**Share Results**
Tap the gold "Share Result" button to capture a screenshot of your compatibility report and send it via any sharing app.

### History
Past compatibility readings are listed below the results, showing partner name, score, and date. Most recent readings appear first.

## 12. The 7 Divination Systems

### Astrology (18% weight)
Western astrology based on your date, time, and place of birth. Calculates your **Sun Sign** (core identity), **Moon Sign** (emotional nature), and **Rising Sign** (outward persona). Daily scores factor in current planetary transits and aspects.

### Numerology (18% weight)
Based on your full name and date of birth:
- **Life Path Number** — Your life's purpose and direction (from DOB)
- **Expression Number** — Your talents and abilities (from full name)
- **Soul Urge Number** — Your inner desires and motivations (from vowels)
- **Personality Number** — How others perceive you (from consonants)
- **Master Numbers** (11, 22, 33) are preserved and flagged as special

### Chinese Zodiac (13% weight)
A 5-element system based on your birth year:
- **Animal Sign** — One of 12 animals (Rat through Pig)
- **Element** — Wood, Fire, Earth, Metal, or Water
- **Polarity** — Yin or Yang
- **Inner Animal** — Based on birth month (hidden personality)
- **Secret Animal** — Based on birth hour (true self)

### Mahabote (17% weight)
Traditional Burmese astrology mapping your birth day to:
- **Day Classification** — One of 7 day types
- **Animal** — Associated spirit animal
- **Planet** — Ruling celestial body
- **Direction** — Auspicious compass direction
Used heavily in Southeast Asian culture for timing decisions and understanding personality.

### Lunar Cycles (14% weight)
Tracks the current phase of the moon and its influence:
- **New Moon** — New beginnings, setting intentions
- **Waxing** — Growth, building, expansion
- **Full Moon** — Peak energy, completion, revelation
- **Waning** — Release, reflection, letting go
Daily score reflects how well the current lunar phase supports different activities.

### Archetypes (10% weight)
Your Jungian archetype from the personality quiz influences:
- Decision-making style recommendations
- Compatibility dynamics with others
- Career and relationship advice framing
- Risk tolerance in financial guidance

### Planetary Hours (10% weight)
An ancient system dividing each day into planetary-ruled hours:
- **7 planets** cycle through 24 hours: Sun, Moon, Mars, Mercury, Jupiter, Venus, Saturn
- **Benefic hours** (Venus, Mercury, Jupiter, Sun) — Favourable for action
- **Malefic hours** (Saturn, Mars) — Better for caution and reflection
- Each hour lasts approximately 60 minutes
- The cycle resets at sunrise each day

## 14. Privacy & Data

### Local-Only Storage
All your data is stored **exclusively on your device**. CosmIQ Guru does not:
- Require an account or login
- Upload your data to any server
- Share your information with third parties
- Track your usage or behaviour

### What's Stored
- Your profile (name, birth details, archetype)
- Zodiac and numerology calculations
- Decision history
- Compatibility results
- Journal entries
- App preferences (notification settings)

### Data Location
- Profile and preferences: SharedPreferences (device local storage)
- History and journal: SQLite database (device local storage)

### Deleting Your Data
Go to **Settings > Clear All Data** to permanently remove everything. This cannot be undone. After clearing, you'll restart the onboarding process.

## 15. Troubleshooting

### App won't open / crashes on launch
- Ensure you have the latest version installed
- Try force-closing and reopening the app
- As a last resort, uninstall and reinstall (this will clear your data)

### Readings seem the same every day
- Readings are based on real astronomical and numerological cycles
- Some days may have similar influences
- Pull down on the Home screen to force a refresh

### Notifications not working
- Check that notifications are enabled in Settings
- Check your device's notification permissions for CosmIQ Guru
- Ensure "Do Not Disturb" mode isn't blocking the notification

### Compatibility shows unexpected results
- Ensure birth details are accurate — even small differences in birth time affect Rising Sign calculations
- If birth time is unknown, use 12:00 PM as an approximation

### Decision Engine scores seem low
- Low scores don't mean "don't do it" — they indicate the timing may not be optimal
- Try different target dates to find more favourable windows
- Check the 7-Day Investment Forecast in Money Mode for better timing
